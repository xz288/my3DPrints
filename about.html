Hi, I'm Blake!

13-Year-Old Tech Enthusiast & Co-Founder

I'm the older brother and I handle the technical side of our business. I love designing 3D models, managing our website, and making sure every order is perfect. I started learning 3D design when I was 11, and it's been an amazing journey!

When I'm not working on 3D prints, you'll find me coding, playing video games, or helping Casper with the more complex prints. I believe technology can change the world, and this business is our way of proving it.

Hi, I'm Casper!

11-Year-Old 3D Printing Expert & Co-Founder

I'm the younger brother and I run the 3D printers! I started when I was 9 because I loved the idea of turning digital designs into real toys you can hold. Every single item in our shop is printed and inspected by me (with Blake's help on the tricky ones).
